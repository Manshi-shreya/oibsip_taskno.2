The Missile Man of India: Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

To succeed in your mission, You must have a single-minded devotion to your goal

-A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Biography

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam is Avul Pakir Jainulabudeen Abdul Kalam was born on 15 October 1931 in Tamil Nadu.He completed his graduation in Physics and then moved on to Madras to study aerospace engineering.After graduating from MIT-Chennai, Kalam joined Aeronautical Development Establishment of DRDO,then he moved to ISRO.On July 27,2015 he left us.

Achievements

Kalam was the Chief Scientific Adviser to the Prime Minister, and the Secretary of DRDO.He also served as the 11th President of India and was honoured with a Bharat Ratna,Padma Bhusan and Padma Vibhusan.He contributed in the desgin of satellite launch vehicle, SLV-III.He was also a very kind hearted person, good mentorand an inspiring author with his simple living and achievements.
